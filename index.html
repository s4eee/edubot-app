Hello! I'd love to help you dive into the fascinating world of **Quantum Physics**. At its heart, it's the study of matter and energy at the most fundamental level.

1. Wave-Particle Duality: Objects can exhibit properties of both particles and waves.

2. Superposition: Particles can exist in multiple states simultaneously until measured.

3. Entanglement: Two particles can become linked, where the state of one instantly influences the other.

For a deep dive, I highly recommend Prof. Allan Adams' MIT course. It's a fantastic primary resource for understanding the experimental basis of quantum mechanics.
